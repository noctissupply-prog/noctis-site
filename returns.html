Returns & Refunds

We aim to keep our customers satisfied. Because we work with third-party vendors and often resell limited items, returns and refunds depend on the product type.

Physical products (Shopify)

If you bought a physical product via our Shopify store, open a ticket in Discord or email arestrot@noctissupply.online within 7 days with order ID and photos. We will advise on return eligibility and shipping instructions.

Digital / Vendor Links (MyAuth)

Digital purchases and vendor links are non-refundable unless proven faulty (wrong link, broken delivery). Submit evidence and we will review within 48 hours.
