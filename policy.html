Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

Noctis Supply respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Contact information: email addresses and Discord tags for support, VIP verification, and order communication.
  • Order data: order IDs and purchase confirmations used to verify access and process disputes.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser user-agent, anonymized analytics to monitor site performance and security.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use data to: process and verify orders, provide support, prevent fraud, communicate updates about purchases and VIP status, and improve the Service. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Third Parties

Payment and delivery processing is handled by third parties (e.g., Shopify, MyAuth). Those providers have their own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their policies.

5. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain personal data only as long as necessary. You can request access, correction or deletion by emailing arestrot@noctissupply.online.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no online system is 100% secure. Report suspected breaches to our contact email immediately.

7. Contact

Questions: arestrot@noctissupply.online
