Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

Noctis Supply values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We operate as a platform connecting buyers with verified vendors and physical products via third-party platforms.

2. Data We Collect

  • Contact information: email addresses and Discord names provided for support and order verification.
  • Order data: order IDs, product purchased, and transaction confirmation (we do not store full payment details).
  • Device & usage data: basic analytics to improve the website (IP address, browser type, anonymous usage data).

3. How We Use Data

We only use data to process support requests, verify VIP purchases, deliver services, and prevent fraud. We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Payments and order processing are handled by third parties such as Shopify and MyAuth. These services have their own privacy policies. Links to those providers are available on request.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services. You may request deletion or correction by contacting arestrot@noctissupply.online.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you, request corrections, or deletion where applicable.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy can be directed to arestrot@noctissupply.online.
