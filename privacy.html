Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Summary

Noctis Supply respects your privacy. We only collect data necessary to process orders, provide support, and verify VIP membership. Payment processing is handled by third parties (Shopify, MyAuth).

2. Data We Collect

  • Contact data: email address, Discord tag for support and VIP verification.
  • Order data: order IDs and purchase confirmations to verify access or process disputes.
  • Technical data: IP addresses, user-agent, anonymized analytics for site health.

3. How We Use Data

To provide services, verify purchases, respond to support requests, and prevent fraud. We do not sell personal data.

4. Third Parties

We use Shopify, MyAuth and other third parties. Their privacy policies apply to payment and vendor link processing.

5. Retention & Rights

Data is stored only as long as needed. You may request access, correction, or deletion by emailing arestrot@noctissupply.online.

Questions: arestrot@noctissupply.online
